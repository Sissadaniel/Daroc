B. Cookies
Attached “Cookie Scan Report” describes the different types of cookies that can be used on elevenandlaw.com (hereinafter the “Website“) and how you can manage these cookies.

Eleven & Law applies a strict privacy policy and is committed to being transparent about the used technologies on the Website.

A “cookie” is a small text file containing information saved by a website on a user’s computer (hereinafter the User). Cookies allow the Website to recognize you and store important information for the purpose of facilitating navigation on the Website, ensuring its proper functioning and/or making it more efficient, for example by memorizing your preferences.  

You may remove, disable or accept Website cookies at any time by configuring your browser settings.

To learn how to delete, disable, or accept, check your browser provider’s web site via the help screen.
